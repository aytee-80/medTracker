Medication Safety Information

Important guidelines to help you use medications safely and effectively.

Important: Never take more than the prescribed dosage. Misuse can result in serious health consequences.

Risks of Overdose

  • Organ damage (liver, kidneys, heart)
  • Severe health complications or death
  • Dangerous drug interactions

Addiction and Dependency

  • Some medications have habit-forming potential
  • Long-term misuse increases dependency risks
  • Always follow your doctor's instructions exactly

Always Consult Your Doctor

This app helps manage your prescriptions, but it does not replace professional medical advice. Always talk to your healthcare provider before changing medication routines, adjusting dosages, or if you experience any side effects.

Emergency Information

If you or someone you know is experiencing a suspected overdose, act immediately.

  • 1
    Call emergency services: 0800 123 456
  • 2
    Keep the person awake and sitting upright if possible
  • 3
    Stay with them until help arrives
  • 4
    Tell responders what medications were taken
